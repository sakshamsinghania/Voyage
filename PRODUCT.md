# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers, data scientists, and technical practitioners doing agentic AI research workflows. They are comfortable with dense UIs, prefer information-forward layouts over hand-holding, and are task-driven: they come to get answers, synthesize information, and move fast. They trust precision over personality.

## Product Purpose

Voyage is an AI agent research chatbot platform. It orchestrates AI agents to help technical users research, synthesize, and explore complex topics. Success looks like a user getting deep, reliable answers faster than they could manually, with full control over the research process.

## Brand Personality

Precise, capable, unobtrusive. Voyage should feel like an expert tool built by people who use it themselves. No ceremony. No warmup. Just sharp, fast, and focused.

## Anti-references

- **ChatGPT / generic AI chat**: bubble conversation UI, centered input box, assistant persona, friendly empty states. Voyage is not a chatbot with a personality.
- **SaaS-cream startup**: rounded card grids, purple-to-blue gradients, hero metrics, "🚀 10x your productivity" copy. Never decorative, never performative.

## Design Principles

1. **Expert defaults**: the interface should feel designed for someone who already knows what they're doing. No onboarding friction in the primary path.
2. **Information density over whitespace theater**: pack meaning into every pixel. Breathing room is earned, not given.
3. **Control is visible**: agents, tools, and state are surfaced, not hidden behind abstractions. Users should always know what's running.
4. **Speed is a feature**: UI response, layout render, and perceived performance are as important as functional correctness.
5. **Neutral surface, opinionated function**: the chrome recedes; the research output commands attention.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast between text and background is non-negotiable for dense information displays. Keyboard navigability throughout. No reliance on color alone to convey state (agent status, error, success).
